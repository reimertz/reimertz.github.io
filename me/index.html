Hi. My name is Piérre Reimertz and I was born in Vänersborg, a tiny town located next to the largest lake in Sweden. There wasn't that much to do there, but somehow, there was a huge music scene and a lot of kids played in bands, me included.

It quickly became obvious that I wouldn't become the next Bob Dylan, so I joined all the other grown-ups at the SAAB car factory as an assembler. The tedious lack of variety slowly turned me into a vegetable. I luckily realized that and left for Australia.

The timing of my homecoming could have been better since the automobile industry had its biggest depression ever, which resulted in me becoming unemployed. But this was, in retrospect, a blessing in disguise since it was the reason I started to take night courses to fix my bad grades so eventually could attend Chalmers University of Technology.

A lot of stuff happened at Chalmers. I feel in love with the creativity of web development, I met a girl and I was lucky to be able to go to San Francisco after I got my Bachelors Degree to work as an web developer intern.

It may sound cliché but in the future when I sit with my grandson or granddaughter in my lap and reflect over my life, I want to be able to tell that I did something to make the world a better place. My work, ideas and entrepreneurship are therefore based on the easy thought of making the world a better place.
